# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users
High school administrators, principals, homeroom teachers (GVCN), and parents looking for high-quality, impactful experiential education and goal-setting programs for THCS (middle school) students. The goal is to build excitement, unity, and a clear sense of purpose for students starting the new school year.

## Product Purpose
To introduce the "Viết Lên Mục Tiêu Năm Học Mới" (Write Your Goals for the New School Year) experiential education program of ABA Center. The landing page must clearly communicate the educational values, the step-by-step timeline, the fun team-building activities, and drive inquiries/bookings.

## Positioning
"Chương trình giáo dục trải nghiệm giúp học sinh THCS bứt phá giới hạn, rèn luyện kỹ năng và tự tin kiến tạo mục tiêu cuộc sống."

## Conversion & proof
- Primary CTA: "Nhận Tư Vấn & Báo Giá"
- Secondary CTA: "Khám Phá Lịch Trình Chi Tiết"
- The line a visitor remembers after 10 seconds: "Truyền cảm hứng cho học sinh thiết lập mục tiêu học tập và cuộc sống thông qua trải nghiệm thực tế đầy năng lượng."
- Belief ladder:
  1. Setting personal goals early in the school year is vital for student motivation and development.
  2. Experiential team-building is the most effective way to teach goal-setting, collaboration, and self-confidence.
  3. ABA Center is a highly professional, expert training provider with specialized programs and equipment.
  4. The timeline is tailored appropriately for each age group (morning for Grades 6-7, afternoon for Grades 8-9).
  5. The program is easy to implement directly at the school's venue.
- Proof on hand: Proven programs deployed at major schools (e.g. Trường TH & THCS Hưng Yên), experienced ABA coaches, premium props, and enthusiastic feedback from schools and students.

## Brand Personality
Inspiring, energetic, professional, educational. It should feel like a high-energy youth event combined with expert pedagogical structure.

## Anti-references
- Dry, corporate SaaS layouts with flat pastel colors and generic icons.
- Childish cartoons (which would alienate THCS/middle school teenagers).
- Overly cluttered, static layouts with hard-to-read, overlapping text.

## Design Principles
1. **Dynamic Energy**: Express the active nature of team-building with smooth, deliberate transitions and warm, energetic colors.
2. **Clear Narrative Flow**: Lead the user from high-level inspiration (Hero) to personal transformation (Core Values), structured execution (Timeline), and visual evidence (Gallery), culminating in a clear CTA.
3. **Structured Clarity**: Present complex schedule tables as interactive, space-efficient tabs that make information digestible and professional.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Clear contrast ratios matching WCAG AA standards.
- Fully responsive layouts optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop viewports.
- Support for `prefers-reduced-motion` to ensure animations degrade gracefully to clean transitions.
